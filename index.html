Routineness vs. Complexity

X-axis (Routineness): how repetitive and rule-based a job is (0 = not routine, 1 = fully routine).
Y-axis (Complexity): how much a job requires judgment and expertise (0 = simple, 1 = highly complex).
Dot size reflects automation probability — bigger dots have higher risk.
Color shows risk level.
Jobs in the bottom-right (high routine, low complex) tend to have the highest automation risk.
Jobs in the top-left (low routine, high complex) tend to be the safest from automation.
